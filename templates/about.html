Med-Minder!

Get your medication routine back on track!


Med-Minder uses your device camera to scan a paper prescription label and populate a text field to help fill out forms. The application then connects to your Google calendar and creates reminders to take the medication which was just scanned. This application is being developed by William Maroney for an Undergraduate Senior Capstone Project.
